Common questions

Do you really take 0% commission?

Yes, always. We charge a flat monthly subscription — nothing more. Every pound your client pays goes directly to you.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard. Your booking page stays active until the end of the billing period.

What is the embeddable widget?

A single line of code you can paste into any website — your own site, Instagram link-in-bio page, or anywhere else. It shows your live booking calendar directly on that page.

How do clients pay?

Clients can pay a deposit at booking or pay in full at the appointment — you choose. Deposits are processed securely via Stripe and paid out directly to you.
